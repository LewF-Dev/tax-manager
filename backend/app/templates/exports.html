Transaction Export (CSV)

Download all your income and expense transactions in CSV format. Suitable for importing into spreadsheets or accounting software.

Full Data Export (JSON)

Download all your data in JSON format. This includes your profile, all transactions, UC reports, and tax snapshots. Required for GDPR compliance.

About Data Exports

  • Exports contain all your data up to the moment you download
  • CSV format is best for spreadsheets and basic analysis
  • JSON format contains complete data including metadata
  • Exports are generated in real-time, not cached
  • Your data is never shared with third parties

Data Portability

You own your data. You can export it at any time, for any reason. If you decide to leave, you can take everything with you.
